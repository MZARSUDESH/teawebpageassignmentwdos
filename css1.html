LION LOGO


What is lion logo

The Lion Logo which appears on Ceylon Tea packs denotes not only the country of origin but also the quality of Ceylon Tea. The Sri Lanka Tea Board is the legal owner of the Ceylon Tea Lion logo which has been licensed in many countries in the world.

The conditions under the lion logo

  • The Lion Logo can be used only on consumer packs of Ceylon Tea
  • The packets must include 100% pure Ceylon Tea Tea.
  • In Sri Lanka, the brands which use the Lion Logo should be packed. Importers/packers from overseas are not approved to use the Lion logo on their tea bags, even though the packs contain pure Ceylon tea
  • The brands that use the Lion Logo shall comply with the Sri Lanka Tea Board's quality requirements.
